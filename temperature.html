Max Temperature

The overall shape of the plot appears to be a flattened upside-down parabola. I see the graph as two linear clusters joined together. All temperatures under the equator tend to rise linearly around 1°F per °Latitude. All temperatures about 20°N tend to fall linearly at 0.75°F per °Latitude. Between the equator and 20°N, the temperatures are around 40°F lower than they would appear to be following the other data. This can be explained by the placement of rainforests.
